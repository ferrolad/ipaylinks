Customer Authentication

iPayLinks adopts e-mail as user authentication accounts. The account is used as the transaction authentication of all kinds via iPayLinks platform.

Networke Transaction Safety

All the products of IPayLinks is referred to the relevant safety recommendations of PCI and OWASP in the process of design and development.

Real-time Monitoring

iPayLinks strictly implements the five-level security management system and keeps on improving it. Two major monitoring departments of iPayLinks, Operation and Risk Control, monitor the operational state in 7*24-hour all year round.

Customers & partners Safety

Strict Onboarding Admittance Standard

iPayLinks sets strict KYC standards to partners. The experienced qualification review team will verify customers' scale of operation and credibility from time to time.

Real-time Anti-money-laundering Surveillance Mechanisms

iPayLinks has established formal anti-money laundering internal control system, and made clear regulations on merchant identification, identification data, transaction record keeping, suspicious transaction reporting, anti-money laundering and financing investigations, etc.

Impeccable Anti-phishing Security System

iPayLinks provides a complete set of anti-phishing security measures in the analysis of transaction monitoring to help consumers recognize phishing and safeguard their account passwords and other important information.
