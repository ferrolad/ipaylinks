Information

iPayLinks Financial Information Service (Shanghai) Co., Ltd (hereafter referred to as "iPayLinks") is a cross-border integrated service provider based on Internet finance and RMB internationalization background, who is committed to providing cross border integrated services and financial services and solutions. The business of iPayLinks covers various fields such as e-commerce, tickets & journey, traditional manufacturing enterprises, conference exhibition, restaurant & hotels, entertainment videos, software, education and games, and keeps on helping merchants improve efficiency and creating values.

iPayLinks focuses on researching the application needs of cross-border business enterprise, developing and promoting integrated service applications system. It is committed to providing enterprises, merchants and e-commerce companies with integrated solutions, solving payment difficulties and keeping on concerning risk control problems. iPayLinks has been certified PCI-DSS security by international credit card payment industry, protecting enterprises’ online transaction security fully.

iPayLinks aims to provide a more comprehensive online service system and service for the whole e-commerce industry. Not only meeting the needs of various business, iPayLinks also insists on providing users with the best experience, improving customer services and determine to become a leader in cross-border services.
